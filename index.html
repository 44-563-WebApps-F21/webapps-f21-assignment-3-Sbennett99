My Favorite Location: Saint Thomas, VI

This location is an Island in the Caribean. It's very beautiful there and the water is crystal clear. Sadly, because of climate change, some of the reefs have actually died, but it is still beautiful none the less.

Directions to my favorite Location

  1. First you must travel from maryville to MCI airport in Kansas City because you you will be flying to this location.
  2. After reaching MCI you will purchase a ticket to Saint Thomas, VI and board your flight.
  3. After Boarding your flight you may sit back and relax until you arrive at your destination.
  4. When you arrive in Saint Thomas you will exit the plane directly onto the tarmac and be required to walk into the aiport from there.
  5. You are now at your destination, so enjoy your stay and all the beauty that there is for you to see.
Things to Bring on your trip
  • Clothes
  • A camera
  • Sunscreen(The UV index is always extremly High)
